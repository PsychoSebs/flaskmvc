The linked blog post speaks to Cyber Bullying, the types of Cyber Bullying, the impact that Cyber Bullying has, as well as how to respond and prevent Cyber Bullying.



What is Cyber Bullying ?


Cyber Bullying is basically bullying which takes place through the use of digital technologies and can occur on social media platforms, messaging platforms, gaming platforms and even mobile phones. It is the repeated behaviour aimed at frightening, angering or even shaming those who are targeted.


Types of Cyber Bullying


Cyber Bullying can take many forms but a few can be noted to be :

Harrassment

Impersonation

Inappropriate Photographs

Website Creation

Video Shaming


Response and Prevention

Responses to Cyber Bullying include:

Report but don't Respond

Document the Indicents

Involve the Police and the School

Be Aware of the Effects


Cyber Bullying can be prevented using a variety of measures but the most popular ones include protecting accounts and devices, maintaining some privacy, practicing thinking before posting, setting limits and performing social media audits.
